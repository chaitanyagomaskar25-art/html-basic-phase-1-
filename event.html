Event Description

Get ready for an inspiring day of ideas, innovation, and interaction! Tech Innovators 2025 is a student-led startup summit that brings together aspiring entrepreneurs, tech enthusiasts, and industry mentors under one roof. The event will feature talks by successful founders, hands-on workshops, and live startup pitching sessions. Whether you’re a beginner or already working on your project, this event will give you insights, connections, and motivation to take your ideas to the next level.
